About the Project

During my internship at AstraZeneca, I gained valuable insights into the critical role IT professionals play in enabling business operations across diverse units. As a Commercial IT Intern, I collaborated with two Governance and Compliance teams to design and develop internal websites that streamlined and accelerated contract review processes. This project challenged me to quickly understand the complexities of governance, compliance, and contract strategies within the healthcare industry—areas in which I had little prior knowledge—and translate them into intuitive, user-friendly resources for employees. By bridging the gap between technical solutions and business needs, I enhanced my skills in web development, problem-solving, and effectively communicating complex ideas in a clear and accessible manner.



Problem Statement

Problem

A lack clear expectations for submitting documents slows down important contract approvals.

Individuals responsible for submitting documents to be reviewed by governance and compliance committees lack a sufficient system for understanding submission requirements. As a result, review and approval of important contracts can be delayed.

Solution

Working with members of two governance and compliance committees, I designed and developed two internal websites for AstraZeneca and Alexion employees with submission templates, contact points, and other helpful resources.

Design Process

Research

User pain points were collected primarily through stakeholder interviews. This also included observational research such as guided walkthroughs of governance and compliance procedures to understand the steps potential users were taking.

⚠️Pain Points

  • Trouble locating important information
  • Need for both consistency and contrast
  • Need for visual indentity
  • Need for longetivity and ease of maintenance

Layout

I worked with governance and compliance employees to identify the most important content to include, and broke these into four sections for each team plus an FAQ section. To organize these sections in a visually appealing way, I leveraged reusable components, including accordion components for ACERT, and "bubbles" for GGC.

Styles/Components

After familiarizing myself with AZ's brand guidelines, I created a style guide to guide the design process and maintain consistency across the sites.

Members of the ACERT team expressed the desire to represent their business units visually. Specific ideas the team wanted to convey included the two distinct sectors of AstraZeneca, BBU/OBU pharmaceutical products and RDU pharmaceutical products, while also highlighting the unity between them. We also experimented with ways to make the business units differ from each other such as through shape and color. The use of round, molecule-like shapes was inspired by one of AstraZeneca's key values: Follow the science.
